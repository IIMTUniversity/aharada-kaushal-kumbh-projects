## About the Event

**Aharada Kaushal Kumbh** is a dynamic AI & Machine Learning innovation sprint that brings together aspiring data scientists, ML engineers, and industry-minded innovators from universities across India.

More than just a hackathon — it is a platform for creativity, problem-solving, and real-world application of artificial intelligence. Participants work on real datasets, build predictive models, and transform ideas into impactful solutions.

This inter-university competition bridges the gap between academic knowledge and industry needs by encouraging analytical thinking, machine learning techniques, and data-driven decision-making.

> "At Aharada Kaushal Kumbh, you don't just build models — you build solutions that shape the future of AI."

## Team Rules & Structure

1. **Team Size:** 3–5 members per team (including the team leader)
2. **Timely Submission:** Submit within the given timeline to qualify
3. **Idea Pivoting:** If the leader's idea is not shortlisted, they may join another team
4. **AI Policy:** AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot) are allowed — but originality is mandatory. Core problem-solving logic must be your own.

## FAQs

**Who can participate?**
Any college student passionate about Data Analytics, ML, and AI. Beginners and experts from all universities are welcome.

**Is there a registration fee?**
No — participation is completely free.

**What should I bring?**
Your college ID card, laptop, chargers, and any specialized hardware for your ML project.

**Are AI tools allowed?**
Yes. AI tools are permitted under the AI Policy. However, the core logic and originality of your project must be your own.
